Is This Right For You?

Questions We Hear Most Often

Honest answers to the real concerns people have before starting.

Do I have to give up my cultural foods — jollof rice, fufu, plantains? ▾

Absolutely not. This is the core of what makes us different. We adapt your traditional foods — we don't eliminate them. We'll show you how to prepare jollof rice, egusi, fufu, plantains, and more in ways that work for your health goals. Your culture is not the problem — and it never will be.

What if I've tried diets before and always failed? ▾

That's the most common thing we hear — and it's not your fault. Generic diets fail people because they ignore who you actually are. Our coaching is not a diet. It's a completely personalized system designed around your specific condition, your food culture, your schedule, and your goals. When the plan fits your real life, you actually follow it.

Will this work for my specific condition? ▾

We specialize in nutrition for chronic conditions including Type 1 & 2 Diabetes, Hypertension, Heart Disease, Kidney Disease, Digestive Disorders, and Weight Management. Your coach is trained in therapeutic nutrition and will tailor everything to your specific labs, medications, and health status. If you're unsure whether we can help, just book a free call — we'll be honest about what's possible.

How do the online coaching sessions work? ▾

We use Zoom for video coaching calls, or phone calls if you prefer. Sessions are completely private and scheduled around your timezone and workday. Many clients book early morning or evenings. Everything is digital — meal plans, recipes, and shopping lists are all delivered via email and WhatsApp.

How long before I start seeing results? ▾

Most clients notice improved energy within 2-3 weeks. Lab improvements (blood sugar, cholesterol, blood pressure) typically appear in 4-8 weeks. Weight loss averages 12-18 lbs in the first 3 months. Sustainable health change takes commitment — and we're here to support you every single step of the way.
