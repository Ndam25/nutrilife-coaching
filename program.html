Is This Right For You?

Questions We Hear Before People Join

Honest answers to the real hesitations people have.

Will I have to give up my cultural foods to participate? ▾

Never. Celebrating and adapting cultural food is at the heart of this program. We hold entire sessions dedicated to West African, Caribbean, and Mediterranean recipes — making them healthier without losing what makes them special. Your food heritage is an asset, not a problem.

What if I've tried group programs before and they didn't work? ▾

Most group programs are one-size-fits-all. This one isn't. You get a personalized meal plan AND culturally adapted guidance AND an individual accountability partner — not just access to group calls. We've designed this specifically for people who've been let down by generic health programs before.

Will this work for my specific condition? ▾

We work with Diabetes (Type 1 & 2), Hypertension, Heart Disease, Kidney Disease, Digestive Disorders, and Weight Management. Your individual meal plan is tailored to your specific condition. If you're unsure whether this program fits your situation, just submit the application and mention it — we'll be upfront about whether we can help.

How much time do I need to commit each week? ▾

The 3 live calls per week run 45-60 minutes each. You can watch replays if you miss one. Beyond that, you'll spend time on meal prep (which you're already doing anyway) and occasional community check-ins. Most members find it fits easily into a busy schedule — the structure actually saves time by eliminating guesswork about what to eat.

What if I don't see results? Is there a guarantee? ▾

Yes. We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If within the first 30 days you've attended the calls, followed the plan, and aren't satisfied with the direction you're heading, we'll work with you to make it right — or provide a refund. We're that confident in the program because we've seen it work consistently across 8+ cohorts.
