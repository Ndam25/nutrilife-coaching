NutriLife Coaching was founded by Dr. Adama NDIR — a Medical Doctor, Senior Epidemiologist, Data Scientist, and IIN-certified Health & Nutrition Coach with 15+ years of expertise in chronic nutritional diseases, African diet, and public health.
