Is this course accredited? Ce cours est-il accrédité ? ¿Este curso está acreditado? ▾

This is an educational course, not a professional nutrition credential. You'll receive a Certificate of Completion suitable for professional portfolios. If you're looking for registered dietitian credentials, consult with the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics for professional pathways.

Es un curso educativo, no una credencial profesional en nutrición. Recibirás un Certificado de Finalización.

Aucune exigence spéciale. Juste un ordinateur, un téléphone ou une tablette avec Internet.
