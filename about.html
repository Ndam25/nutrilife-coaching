The Story Behind
NutriLife Coaching L'Histoire Derrière
NutriLife Coaching La Historia Detrás de
NutriLife Coaching

Growing up in West Africa, I watched family members and members of my community struggle with diabetes, hypertension, and other chronic diseases. Many were eating "modern" processed foods while losing connection to the healing power of traditional foods. En grandissant en Afrique de l'Ouest, j'ai vu des membres de la famille et de ma communauté lutter contre le diabète, l'hypertension et d'autres maladies chroniques. Creciendo en África Occidental, vi a miembros de la familia y de mi comunidad luchar contra la diabetes, la hipertensión y otras enfermedades crónicas.

As a physician and nutritionist, I dedicated my career to finding a better way — one that honors our food heritage while applying the most rigorous nutritional science. With a background spanning medicine, epidemiology, and data science, I bring an evidence-based approach that respects your culture, your foods, and your life. Today, I am passionate about helping the diaspora reconnect with their healing foods while thriving in their current location. En tant que médecin et nutritionniste, j'ai dédié ma carrière à trouver une meilleure voie — une qui honore notre patrimoine alimentaire tout en appliquant la science nutritionnelle la plus rigoureuse. Como médico y nutricionista, dediqué mi carrera a encontrar un mejor camino — uno que honre nuestra herencia alimentaria mientras aplica la ciencia nutricional más rigurosa.
